This is a fix for Intel HD 3000 and legacy NVIDIA graphics for macOS Catalina 10.15.+.

Supported: Intel HD Graphics 3000; GeForce 5xx, 4xx, 2xx, 8600M(GT), 8800M(GT), 9400M, 9600M(GT), 320M and 330M.

Kernel extensions to be installed or modified:

AppleIntelHD3000Graphics.kext
AppleIntelHD3000GraphicsGA.plugin
AppleIntelHD3000GraphicsGLDriver.bundle
AppleIntelHD3000GraphicsVADriver.bundle
AppleIntelSNBGraphicsFB.kext
AppleIntelSNBVA.bundle

GeForceTesla.kext
GeForceTeslaGLDriver.bundle
GeForceTeslaVADriver.bundle
NDRVShim.kext
NVDANV50HalTesla.kext
NVDAResmanTesla.kext

IOHIDFamily.kext
IOSurface.kext


PrivateFrameworks to be modified:

GPUSupport.framework
SkyLight.framework


Frameworks to be modified:
OpenGL.framework
CoreDisplay.framework
